# Work Packages and Milestones

All numeric geometry below is a scenario range derived from later estimates, not a calibrated reconstruction. Model work is intended to reveal assumptions and discriminating tests, not to confirm a preferred explanation.

## WP-01 — Canonical AMT-01 geometry

**Goal:** define a parameterized body rather than one fixed reconstruction.

Parameters:

```text
L = 6–8 m
W = 2.5–3 m
H = 0.1–1.0 m scenario range
S = 7.5–12 m²
mass test scenarios = 20, 50, 100, 300, 500, 1000 kg (mass is unknown)
```

Deliverable: coordinate model and scenario table.

## WP-02 — Force Skin control model

**Goal:** solve for force distributions that support, accelerate, and stabilize the body.

Deliverables:

- hover solution;
- pitch/roll/yaw control basis functions;
- wind disturbance rejection;
- control-bandwidth requirements;
- comparison with rotorcraft signatures.

Milestone: a numerical simulation of the stated conceptual requirements, accompanied by sensitivity analysis and comparison with conventional control architectures.

## WP-03 — Momentum and pressure ledger

**Goal:** make conservation accounting explicit.

Deliverables:

- support momentum reservoir alternatives;
- boundary pressure stress;
- field energy/stress placeholders;
- loss and heat channels;
- list of candidate mechanisms conditionally disfavored by required field intensity or predicted side effects.

## WP-04 — Q-MEVE moving-boundary mathematics

**Goal:** represent a moving forbidden region with precursor and relaxation gradients.

Deliverables:

- \(\Omega(t)\) geometry;
- \(U(x,t)\) family;
- penetration criterion;
- gradient length vs velocity;
- shock-avoidance condition;
- wake restoration stability.

## WP-05 — Black-window sensor model

**Goal:** test whether known spectral/optical behavior can reproduce the remembered appearance.

Deliverables:

- \(A+R+T=1\) band tables;
- visible-black/IR-transparent cases;
- AR/high-transmission cases;
- optical-trap cases;
- sensor and field-aperture layout hypotheses.

## WP-06 — High-speed and underwater scaling

**Goal:** quantify the minimum requirements at Mach/hydrodynamic regimes.

Deliverables:

- dynamic-pressure tables by altitude/depth;
- normal interaction power scale;
- precursor distances;
- expected plasma, acoustic, cavitation, and wake signatures;
- separation of high-altitude and low-altitude claims.

## WP-07 — External case matrix

**Goal:** compare official cases using physical signatures and evidence quality.

Deliverables:

- verified source page;
- event geometry;
- signature vector;
- mundane alternatives;
- tier and relevance score.

## WP-08 — Research governance

**Goal:** keep the knowledge base coherent.

Deliverables:

- monthly claim-ledger review;
- decision records;
- model versioning;
- master dossier regeneration;
- archived superseded assumptions.
