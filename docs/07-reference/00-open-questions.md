# Open Questions

These are research prompts, not claims that the named systems exist or caused the reported event.

## AMT-01 case

1. What was the exact day, month, and site within the recalled summer 2004 timeframe?
2. What were the exact lamp height, spacing, and path geometry?
3. What was the object's thickness and internal volume?
4. What was its mass?
5. Were the black regions flush windows, recessed traps, or openings?
6. What wavelengths would reveal the main surface and windows differently?
7. Was any infrared, RF, magnetic, or electrostatic signature present but unmeasured?

## Force Skin

8. What physical reservoir receives the reaction momentum?
9. Can a distributed support force remain silent and low-loss at the required scale?
10. What control bandwidth would a hypothetical distributed-support model require to reproduce motion that appeared stable in the recalled shore wind?
11. Does the main force act on the surface, body volume, Earth, or surrounding field?

## MEVE

12. How is near-vacuum maintained against atmospheric or hydrostatic pressure?
13. Why does the outer boundary not act as a hard moving obstacle?
14. How is pressure stress transmitted without obvious side effects?
15. How is the wake closed without shock, cavitation, or turbulence?

## Q-MEVE

16. What field can exclude neutral molecules, droplets, dust, and ions universally?
17. Is the wavefunction/potential language merely an analogy, or can it be mapped to a field theory?
18. What are the minimum energy and entropy costs of moving the exclusion region?
19. How is momentum distributed over space and time?
20. What distinct observable signature separates Q-MEVE from plasma or ordinary pressure control?

## High-speed and transmedium

21. Are reported extreme speeds independently constrained or products of range/parallax error?
22. At what altitude did any reported 20,000–30,000 km/h motion occur?
23. Is there preserved audio that should contain a sonic boom?
24. Are there verified water-entry cases with known geometry and no splash/wake?
25. Does the same object class actually operate in all media?

## Control and origin

26. Is the system fully autonomous, locally task-controlled, or remotely operated?
27. What sensor suite corresponds to the black-window topology?
28. Does a carrier/daughter-craft architecture fit available reports?
29. Is a warp/metric layer required, or are long-duration sublight probes sufficient?
30. What observation would distinguish human advanced technology from non-human origin?
