## Derivative rules

1. Historical packages remain immutable.
2. Public corrections are additive and link to the record they supersede or refine.
3. Local machine paths are not provenance identifiers and are never published.
4. Source hashes establish package identity, not factual accuracy.
5. Generated compilations are published only when their generator and drift check are committed.
6. Private or rights-uncleared assets are not copied into the public repository.
