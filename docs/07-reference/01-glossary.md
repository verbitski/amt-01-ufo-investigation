# Glossary

**AMT-01** — project identifier for the retrospective Antalya-Mediterranean witness case record.

**Aperture-like zone** — neutral geometric term for an AMT-01 black region.

**Black region** — neutral term for a remembered sharply bounded region that appeared completely black, with no reflection visible to the witness. **Black window** is a hypothesis-bearing term and should be used only when discussing the aperture hypothesis.

**Boundary stress shell** — hypothetical field structure carrying external pressure around a MEVE cavity.

**Downwash** — downward airflow used by rotors/fans to generate lift.

**EHD** — electrohydrodynamic propulsion, often called ion-wind propulsion.

**Force Skin** — project-developed conceptual requirements model for distributed support and control; not an observed interface or established technology.

**Functional window** — hypothetical sealed region transparent or active in a selected spectral or field band.

**Level-3 interaction** — model in which admissible states or coupling of surrounding matter are altered before physical contact.

**Low-observable** — designed or naturally configured for low detection in optical, acoustic, thermal, radar, or other channels.

**MEVE** — Matter-Exclusion Vacuum Envelope, a speculative project model.

**Precursor field** — hypothetical region acting ahead of the craft to prepare or exclude the medium before contact.

**Q-MEVE** — highly speculative field or quantum-like extension of MEVE using an effective exclusion boundary.

**Reaction reservoir** — system that receives equal/opposite momentum.

**Spectrally selective** — having different absorption, reflection, and transmission in different wavelength bands.

**Transmedium** — capable of operating across different physical media, especially air and water.

**Wake-restoration field** — hypothetical region that returns the medium behind the craft toward normal conditions.

**Warp/metric transport** — hypothetical spacetime-geometry manipulation, distinct from local matter exclusion.
