# Q-MEVE — Level-3 Matter Exclusion

> **Conceptual sandbox:** This document explores a highly speculative analogy. It is not an observation, recovered technology, established physics, or evidence of what caused the reported AMT-01 event.

## Origin of the idea

The project considered whether the boundary could create a quantum-fluid-like region in which matter lacks a definite local position and therefore does not contact the hull.

The refined version is more precise:

> In the abstraction, an assumed envelope would impose effective field or quantum-like boundary conditions that make a protected region inaccessible to external matter.

This is not the same as saying that ordinary air or water becomes a room-temperature Bose–Einstein condensate or literally ceases to exist.

## Level definitions

```text
Level 1 — ordinary material contact
Level 2 — physical cavity, gas layer, plasma layer, or supercavitation
Level 3 — admissible states/coupling of matter are changed before contact
```

## Effective-potential model

Let the protected region be \(\Omega(t)\). Introduce an effective potential:

\[U(\mathbf{x},t)\]

A surrounding particle experiences:

\[\mathbf{F}=-\nabla U\]

The desired boundary condition is symbolically:

\[|\psi(\mathbf{x},t)|^2\rightarrow0\]

for:

\[\mathbf{x}\in\Omega(t)\]

This notation is a conceptual model, not a demonstrated macroscopic technology.

## Tunneling-style intuition

For a simple barrier, transmission falls approximately as:

\[T\sim e^{-2\kappa d}\]

with:

\[\kappa=\frac{\sqrt{2m(U-E)}}{\hbar}\]

A sufficiently strong/wide barrier makes penetration negligible. The challenge is identifying a real field that can apply universally to neutral molecules, droplets, ions, dust, and macroscopic matter without destructive side effects.

## Momentum conservation

Any physical version of the model could not make momentum disappear:

\[\frac{d}{dt}(P_{craft}+P_{medium}+P_{field}+P_{source})=0\]

Possible transfer channels:

- broad surrounding medium;
- field stress;
- energy/momentum source;
- Earth or a larger inertial/gravitational reservoir;
- radiation;
- unknown coupling.

Within the abstraction, the objective would be to prevent **local violent** momentum exchange, not conservation itself.

## Precursor and wake

A hypothesized shock-free moving exclusion region would require:

```text
future path boundary forms ahead
→ surrounding matter is redirected or becomes excluded
→ inner hull follows through the prepared region
→ boundary relaxes behind
```

If the boundary is abrupt, it remains a moving wall and should produce shock. The gradient length and activation timing are therefore central model variables.

## Primary unresolved issue

No established field is known to provide universal, low-signature exclusion of neutral matter at macroscopic scale. Q-MEVE is retained only as a disciplined unknown-mechanism category, not as a solved quantum theory or an explanation supported by the witness account.
