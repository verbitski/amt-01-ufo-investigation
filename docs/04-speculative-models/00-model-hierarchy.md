# Model Hierarchy

> **Conceptual sandbox:** This document explores conditional models. It is not an observation, recovered technology, established physics, or evidence of what caused the reported AMT-01 event.

The project organizes its thought models in layers so that a conditional mechanism proposed for one function is not assumed to solve every scale of travel.

```text
Layer 0 — Reported physical form and black regions; functional-window hypothesis
Layer 1 — Force Skin: low-speed support, attitude, translation
Layer 2 — MEVE: near-hull matter exclusion and pressure boundary
Layer 3 — Q-MEVE: precursor/exclusion coupling that avoids hard contact
Layer 4 — Inertial envelope: high-acceleration load management
Layer 5 — Metric/warp-adjacent transport: long-distance displacement
Layer 6 — Mission autonomy and strategic control
```

## Relationship to the reported AMT-01 account

- Layer 0: the witness recalls an underside, body contour, and black regions; their physical nature and function are not established.
- Layer 1: project-defined requirements abstraction for the reported low-speed motion.
- Layer 2: speculative extension motivated by the reported lack of obvious interaction and by separate transmedium claims.
- Layer 3: highly speculative solution to shock and boundary problems.
- Layer 4: not required by the slow motion described in the account.
- Layer 5: not reported in AMT-01.
- Layer 6: conditional control architecture, not something the witness could observe.

## Important separation

A hypothetical system could use one proposed layer without using another. The layers are related only by the functions they are intended to explore; the project does not claim that any layer exists or that they form one field.
