# Hypothesis Registry

This registry organizes explanations to test. A status describes the project's current analytical treatment, not truth, impossibility, or probability.

## Status vocabulary

- **Open:** remains a live explanation family because current evidence cannot eliminate it.
- **Conditionally disfavored:** conflicts with parts of the witness record under stated estimates or detection assumptions, but is not eliminated.
- **Exploratory requirements model:** organizes a functional problem without identifying a physical mechanism.
- **Speculative sandbox:** extends beyond available evidence or known demonstrated mechanisms.
- **Outside case scope:** may matter to other reports but is not required by AMT-01.

## Model-selection rule

No explanation becomes preferred merely because another has a weakness. Comparison must use the same witness record, estimate ranges, detection conditions, and burden of prediction. Conventional and perceptual alternatives remain open until evidence or a reproducible calculation constrains them more strongly.
