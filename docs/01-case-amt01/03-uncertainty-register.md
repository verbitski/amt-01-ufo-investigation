# AMT-01 Uncertainty Register

Uncertainty is recorded to improve engineering discipline, not to negate the observation.

| Exact hotel/location | Unknown | Prevents precise 3D site reconstruction |

## Highest-value future recovery items

If the exact site or date becomes recoverable, prioritize:

1. lamp height and spacing;
2. path geometry;
3. observer position;
4. historical hotel surveillance availability;
5. weather and wind direction;
6. photographs of the unchanged site.
