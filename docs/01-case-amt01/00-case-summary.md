# AMT-01 Case Summary

## Designation

- **Case:** AMT-01
- **Expanded name:** Antalya-Mediterranean Triangle 01
- **Evidence:** one retrospective first-person witness record and later reconstruction material
- **Location:** Mediterranean coast of Turkey, near Antalya; exact site not yet reconstructed
- **Event period:** summer 2004; exact day and month unknown
- **Approximate time:** 21:00–22:00

## Compact description

The witness recalls a rigid-looking elongated triangular form moving nose-first along the coastline. Later visual estimates place it at roughly 6–8 m long, 2.5–3 m wide, 4–5 m above the local ground or water, and approximately 10–15 km/h. Its underside looked flat, continuous, dark, and matte. Roughly 5–7 irregular regions appeared completely black, with no reflection visible to the witness. No obvious sound, downwash, exhaust, glow, odor, pressure sensation, oscillation, or environmental reaction was noticed under the recalled conditions.

## Reported constraints used for analysis

1. **Low remembered speed:** later estimates imply low dynamic pressure if the scale estimate is approximately correct.
2. **Low remembered height:** later estimates make expected noise and airflow useful comparison questions for air-moving mechanisms.
3. **Stable-looking motion:** no obvious flutter, banking, wobble, or course correction was noticed.
4. **No visible propulsion:** no rotors, nozzles, jets, or ducts were noticed at the available visual resolution.
5. **Low visibility outside lamp illumination:** the form fell below visibility after leaving the lit area.
6. **Unusually dark regions:** several bounded areas looked completely black compared with the surrounding underside.

## Main unknowns

- exact day, month, and site;
- mass;
- thickness and upper geometry;
- exact position, size, and nature of black regions;
- energy source;
- support mechanism;
- operator or origin.

## Current analytical position

AMT-01 remains unresolved. The project translates the reported features into conditional requirements and compares conventional, perceptual, environmental, and speculative explanations. No platform, origin, material, or mechanism has been established.
