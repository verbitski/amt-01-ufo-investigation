# Project Charter

## Mission

Preserve and examine the AMT-01 witness record while maintaining strict separation between recollection, later estimate, derivation, inference, external source, and speculation. Translate reported features into explicit functional questions that alternative explanations can be tested against.

## Primary objective

Develop the smallest set of clearly stated requirements and competing explanations capable of addressing the reported AMT-01 feature set:

```text
large rigid triangular body
+ very low altitude
+ very low speed
+ no visible conventional propulsion noticed
+ no obvious sound noticed
+ no obvious downwash noticed
+ no visible environmental interaction noticed
+ unusually low remembered optical return
+ apparently stable linear motion
```

## Secondary objectives

- Compare AMT-01 with public UAP records without assuming identity.
- Test known physical mechanisms under stated parameter assumptions and record where uncertainty prevents exclusion.
- Convert speculative ideas into equations, predictions, and falsifiers.
- Define a safe theoretical and experimental research program.
- Preserve project knowledge in a versioned, auditable format.

## Non-goals

The project does not currently claim to:

- prove extraterrestrial origin;
- identify the manufacturer or operator;
- prove a connection to Navy or PURSUE cases;
- prove warp travel, antigravity, inertial cancellation, or quantum communication;
- construct a flight-capable device;
- perform high-energy, high-voltage, plasma, radiation, or hazardous experiments;
- treat every unresolved public report as evidence of one technology.

## Working assumptions

1. AMT-01 is accepted as the internal modeling baseline, not as authenticated ground truth.
2. Explicitly uncertain memories remain marked uncertain.
3. Known conservation laws remain in force unless a model produces a testable alternative.
4. “No observed signature” means below the witness's detection threshold, not mathematically zero.
5. A model is preferred when it explains more observations with fewer independent assumptions.
6. Public records are comparative material, not automatic validation.

## Core method

### Constraint-first reverse engineering

Instead of naming a propulsion system and fitting the observation to it, begin with mandatory functions:

- support weight;
- maintain attitude;
- reject wind disturbance;
- translate horizontally;
- avoid or suppress acoustic, aerodynamic, thermal, and optical signatures;
- sense the environment;
- supply energy and control.

### Hypothesis registry

Every candidate mechanism receives:

- a status;
- compatible observations;
- conflicts;
- required signatures;
- discriminating tests;
- update conditions.

### Model hierarchy

Models are layered so that a low-speed explanation is not automatically treated as a theory of interstellar transport.

## Success criteria for the next project phase

The next phase is successful when it produces:

1. a versioned witness record with additive corrections and explicit uncertainty;
2. reproducible calculations with sensitivity to distance, scale, timing, mass, wind, and detection thresholds;
3. a comparison matrix that keeps perceptual, conventional, environmental, and speculative explanations open under the same rules;
4. source verification against original material rather than AI summaries or transcription alone;
5. documented predictions and failure conditions for every retained model;
6. public contribution and review rules that protect privacy and provenance.
